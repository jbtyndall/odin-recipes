French Toast

Description

There are many, fancy variations on this basic recipe. This works with many types of bread: white, whole wheat, cinnamon-raison, Italian, or French. Server hot with butter or margarine and maple syrup!

Ingredients

  • 6 thick slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • 1/4 teaspon ground nutmeg (Optional)
  • 1 teaspon vanilla extract (Optional)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Beat together eggs, milk, salt, desired spices, and vanilla
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Dunk each slice of bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides.
  4. Place in pan, and cook on both sides until golden. Serve hot.
