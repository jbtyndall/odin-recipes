Peanut Butter & Banana Sandwiches

Description

This recipe is fit for a king!

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana
  • 2 slices bread, any kind will do
  • 2 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Cut banana into slices.
  2. Lightly toast the bread.
  3. Spread the peanut butter on one piece and layer the banana on top of the peanut butter.
  4. Fry the sandwich in the butter, turning once until each side is golden brown.
  5. Remove to a plate, then cut the sandwich carefully in half on the diagonal and enjoy!
